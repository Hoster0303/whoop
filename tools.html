Recovery Tracking

Our advanced watch technology provides comprehensive insights into your body's recovery process. By analyzing key metrics such as Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Resting Heart Rate (RHR), and Respiratory Rate, it helps you understand how well your body is recuperating from daily stress and workouts. This data empowers you to make informed decisions about your training and rest, ensuring optimal performance and health.

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Resting Heart Rate (RHR)
  • Respiratory Rate

Strain Monitoring

Our watch continuously measures your cardiovascular load throughout the day, providing valuable insights to optimize your training regimen. By tracking metrics such as Daily Exertion Score, Training Load, and Cardiovascular Strain, it helps you balance your workouts and recovery, ensuring you train effectively without overexertion. This feature is essential for athletes and fitness enthusiasts aiming to maximize performance and minimize injury risk.

  • Daily Exertion Score
  • Training Load
  • Cardiovascular Strain
